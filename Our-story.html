Our Story

Hearth & Honey was born out of Nya’s love for slow living, good tea, and the peaceful rhythms of nature. After spending years exploring new places and embracing the simple joys of community, she returned to her hometown of Bayfield, WI, ready to create a space where people could connect with each other and the world around them. Whether you’re here for a cup of tea, a session in the sauna, or just to relax by Lake Superior, Hearth & Honey is all about taking a moment to unwind, be present, and enjoy life at a slower pace.
